## Research: What Works Best

### Pricing Psychology

1. **3 tiers is optimal** — ✅ We have this
2. **Middle tier should be "Most Popular"** — We marked Growth as popular ✅
3. **Price anchoring** — Put highest price first to anchor
4. **Round numbers** — Work well for services

### Service Business Pricing Models

| Model | Pros | Cons |
|-------|------|------|
| Fixed Price | Predictable, clear | Scope creep risk |
| Hourly | Fair, flexible | Client resistance |
| Retainer | Steady income | Commitment required |
| Value-based | High margin | Harder to quantify |

---

## Recommendations

### Keep 3 Tiers, Adjust:

1. **Quick Fix** → €750-1,000
   - 1-2 week delivery
   - "Get started fast"

2. **Growth** → €2,500-3,500  
   - Most popular
   - 2-4 week delivery
   - "Best value"

3. **Enterprise** → €5,000+
   - Custom scope
   - "Let's talk"

### Add:
- Clear delivery timelines
- What ISN'T included
- Revision rounds
